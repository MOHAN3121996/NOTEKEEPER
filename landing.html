About Notes Keeper

Notes Keeper was designed with modern users in mind. We understand the need for a clean, efficient, and secure note-taking solution that works across all your devices.

Our mission is to help you capture and organize your thoughts, ideas, and important information without the clutter and complexity of traditional note apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Notes Keeper free to use?

Yes, Notes Keeper is completely free to use with all features available to all users.

How secure are my notes?

Your notes are secured with user authentication. Only you can access your notes when logged into your account.

Can I access my notes offline?

Currently, Notes Keeper requires an internet connection to access your notes.

How do I export my notes?

You can export your notes as text or PDF files using the export buttons in the navigation bar.
